    About

    • This library is situated at first floor of Block-C building.
    • The Library services can be used by Students and Faculties of college for Academic and Research purpose. Students can register for Library facility at the start of the semester.
    • Library aims to provide books, reference books and magazines to Students and Faculties in a way that they do not need to buy any books during their stay at the college.
    • The library has approximate 21834 number of volumes which covers almost all text book and reference book listed in GTU syllabus.

    Services

    Presently Library has more than 21000 books, leading national and international journals, magazines and newspapers serves as a platform for knowledge sharing for all staff and students of this institute. We have reading room available for students with furniture.
